02 · Typography

The voice

Georgia for serif headlines and italics. Inter for sans-serif body and eyebrows. Italics carry the accent colour.

t-heroGeorgia 700 · 92px

A bold question in italics.

t-h2Georgia 700 · 52px

The section heading style.

t-ledeGeorgia italic · 28px

A muted italic lede sets the tone — introspective, considered, in scale of voice.

t-deckGeorgia italic · 22px

The deck — a one-line summary that follows a section heading.

t-eyebrowInter 700 · 11px tracked
Pre-headline label
t-bodyInter 400 · 18px / 1.7

Body copy in Inter sans. Generous line-height to keep the page calm. Bold for emphasis returns the text to ink-black; italic accents use the gold tone.

t-body t-dropcapDrop-cap variant

Drop caps in serif gold mark the opening of major sections. They reset the visual rhythm and signal that something new begins here — a chapter, a turn, a fresh argument worth following through.

04 · Pull quotes

The accent voice

Italic callouts with a coloured rule. Use sparingly — after an opening claim or before a turn in the argument.

"We don't need to acquire better customers. We need to create better first purchases."
"The 30 days after a first purchase are worth more than the 30 days before."
"Our most loyal users are not churning. They are drifting. And we are letting them."
05 · Cards

Containers with meaning

Each card carries a semantic accent. Gold for opportunity, sage for healthy, blush for risk, ink for decisions.

Bright spot

A signal worth amplifying.

350,000 K-beauty loyalists retain at 54–55% — above platform average. This is a brand cluster Tira can protect and grow.

Healthy state

The premiumizers.

74,000 users have doubled their ASP — from ₹738 to ₹1,498. Real organic premiumization, already in motion.

Risk · silent leak

The downgraders.

43% of loyal users are buying cheaper over time. ₹1,401 → ₹769 per item. Not churning — quietly trading down.

Decision · Critical

Protect the ₹1,000 threshold.

Bundle prompts, guided discovery, free-delivery breakpoints. The single highest-ROI gate on the platform.

06 · Charts

The data frame

Inline SVG charts live inside a paper-coloured frame with title, italic subtitle, and a footnote.

07 · Closing band

The end of the argument

A full-width dark band closes a document. Radial gold wash for warmth.

The thesis · in one sentence

The next chapter is not about acquiring more.
It is about getting more out of what we already have.

The premium customer is not a future aspiration — she is already in the data, hidden in plain sight. The platform's job, beginning now, is to recognise her, protect her, and build the platform around her.
